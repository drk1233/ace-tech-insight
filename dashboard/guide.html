Ace Tech Insight Manual

Your complete guide to managing the future of tech reporting.

Writing & Publishing

The Write Post editor is where the magic happens. Designed for distraction-free writing, it comes equipped with powerful integrations.

  • AI Assistant Panel: Highlight text in the editor to deploy Claude AI. You can instantly rewrite, expand, or fix grammar. Custom prompts let you generate entirely new sections.
  • Gemini SEO Optimizer: Enter a Focus Keyword at the bottom. The AI generates target word counts, reveals competitor titles, and provides a real-time checklist of required NLP terms. Green terms are injected successfully; Red terms are missing.
  • Unsplash Integration: Click the Image Icon or "Set Cover Image" to search millions of high-res photos. Clicking one instantly adds it to your post with proper photographer attribution!

Managing Content

Control your entire library from the All Posts tab.

  • Quick Filters: Toggle between Drafts, Published, and Scheduled posts instantly using the top ribbons.
  • Bulk Actions: Check the boxes next to your posts to use the Bulk Actions dropdown engine. You can mass-publish, delete, or change the category of 10+ posts simultaneously.
  • Duplicate: Hit the duplicate button to instantly clone an existing post's rich text into a new draft.
Safety Feature: In the Categories Manager, you cannot delete a Category or Tag if an active post is currently using it!

Growth & Analytics

Track your pulse under the Analytics tab.

  • Traffic Validation: The core charts (Views & Subscribers) map automatically to the timeframe you select (7, 30, 90, All-time).
  • Keyword Tracking: In the Keywords tab, monitor "breakout" tech terms. See their 0-100 Trend Velocity metric alongside estimated Search Volume. Click the Create Post button next to any suggested keyword idea to instantly launch a new Editor draft primed for that topic!

Community Management

Maintain the health of your blog interactions.

  • Comments Moderation: New comments from the public blog filter directly into the Pending queue. Pending remarks are intentionally frozen at the top of the table until you explicitly click Approve or Reject. They will not appear live until approved.
  • Premium Paywalls: If you toggle "Premium" on a post in the Editor, the system automatically blurs the text and inserts a lock screen. Only active emails housed in your Subscribers Manager table can view it.

Monetization & Ads

Turn views into revenue.

  • Sponsorship Pipeline: Drag and drop prospective brand deals across the Kanban board from "Inbound" to "Negotiation" to "Active".
  • Global Ad Toggles: Quickly flip the switches to turn off/on sidebar display ads or in-article native ads across the whole public network instantly!
